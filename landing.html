Our Menu

Chocolate Cake

Chocolate cake or chocolate gâteau (from French: gâteau au chocolat) is a cake flavored with melted chocolate, cocoa powder, or both. It can also have other ingredients such as fudge, vanilla creme, and other sweeteners.

Strawberry Cake

Strawberry cakes may be prepared with strawberries in the batter, with strawberries atop them, with strawberries or a strawberry filling in between the layers of a layer cake, and in any combination thereof. Some are prepared with strawberries incorporated into a frosting. Fresh or frozen strawberries may be used.

Butterscotch Cake

This butterscotch cake is a fluffy and moist brown butter, brown sugar cake with a hint of cinnamon and frosted with a luxurious whipped butterscotch ganache. Top with a butterscotch drip and a pinch of sea salt flakes
